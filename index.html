About JRI ImpEx

JRI ImpEx is a registered agricultural export company based in India, specializing in sourcing and exporting premium quality farm produce to international markets worldwide.

We export fresh vegetables and agri commodities including onions and coconuts — ensuring export grade quality, proper documentation, and timely delivery to buyers across the globe.

With direct connections to farmers and a strong logistics network, we deliver farm-fresh produce that meets international quality standards and buyer specifications.
